#### Backstory

Spend his early youth sneaking and skulking in the [[Amber Reach]], playing messenger between different settlements. Eventually got into a job called liberator for some powerful enduring in the network of [[The Damask Queens]]. A liberator is a person who seeks and liberates old relics from places deep within the [[Halcyon Domain]]. It started out as an honest job, going to parts of the [[Amber Reach]] and liberation some items and relics of old temples, dead corpses,... .Yet little by little, honesty gave way to greed, and the job slipped into corruption. [[The Damask Queens]] wanted Skivnn and his dear frend Pablov eskrowba to steal relics from the [[Celenium Athenium]]. 
That night they ventured through the Ashcall Quarters unseen into the [[Celenium Athenium]]. Their target the Central Pillar. A ginormous pillar filled with books and relics, and at its top the sacred pyre protecting the [[Celenium Athenium]]. This pilar is the hart of this settlement. Where people come to read in the relics they liberated from all over the [[Halcyon Domain]].  Unseen they got to the base of the tower, scaled the outsides to a window on the second floor.
