#### Backstory

Mahmoud, a vessel for a long forgotten god [[Shub'Niggurath]]. This god was part of the first gods a thousand years before the [[apostasy]].
When the war between the first gods, [[The Reforming ]]and people of the [[Halcyon Domain]] ended, [[Shub'Niggurath]] left, knowing that giving the humanoid more power will only come with more destruction. After he left all his vessels stopped working inactive. Later temples were reformed to worship other gods. Starting the age of the first kings, a prosperous time for the [[Halcyon Domain]]. 
2 thousand years after the vessel first stopped working a new energy flew into it. Mahmoud not knowing what happened

Mahmoud awoke after 2 thousand years of inactivity not knowing by who or what,... 
The spirit gave him one purpose finding other old relics and vessels of the god [[Shub'Niggurath]]. And reigniting the flame in them.


During his roaming around this estranged world now turned darker and more evil then he remembered, he bestowed upon a fellow traveller. An old Scholar who delved in the Mines close to Okros. He wanted to start a new life, after his Daughter had died in the first uncovering of the [[Shalk Shasm]]. This man called [[Almeas]], was on his way to [[Zandpunt]] when he got ambushed by [[Netherbears]], Mahmoud heard the cries for help and was able with his divine power to hold the creatures of and free [[Almeas]]. Mahmoud told all about his previous life to [[Almeas]] and he, a scholar of Okros was trilled to learn Mahmoud is a vessel from before the old world. Now [[Almeas]] helps Mahmoud with finding new Clanks and other Vessels and often cares for them. Together they search a way to help the spectres get back into the vessel.
